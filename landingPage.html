Little Angels Public School is one of the best private institutions in Abbottabad. LAPS is a world class academic institution with a proud history of achievements and ambitious plans for the future. Little Angels Public School provide quality education from early years till high school. It focuses on nurturing the individual holistically by implanting in them, a balanced approach towards life. Consistent with our national values, we seek to develop the hidden potentials to excel and persevere in meeting the challenges they have to encounter in life by grooming them intellectually and to make them morally strong and effective global citizens.
